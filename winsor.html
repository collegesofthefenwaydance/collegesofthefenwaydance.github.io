Winsor School Dance Studio

103 Pilgrim Rd., Boston, MA 02215



Directions:

  • Located on the first floor of the Lubin O'Donnell Center for Performing Arts.
  • Walk to the end of Pilgrim Road behind the Simmons residence campus.
  • ALTERNATE ROUTE: From Brookline Ave., walk alongside the small alleyway next to the Holmes Sports Center.

  • At the entrance to the performing arts center, ring the doorbell located on your right.
  • Inform the security guard that you are here for COF Dance.
  • Take a left turn at the security desk and walk towards the staircase.
  • The dance studio is located on your left behind the glass windows.

Features:

  • Sprung marley dance floor
  • Sound system with Bluetooth
  • Ballet barres, wall-mounted
  • Front mirrors and curtains

You MUST bring your student IDs with you to check in with security at the front desk!
