Saint Julie Hall Dance Studio

Emmanuel College



Directions:

  • Located at the rear of Saint Julie Hall on the Emmanuel Campus.
  • Check in at the front desk and drop off your student ID.
  • Walk towards the rear of the building past the MCPHS elevators.
  • The dance studio is behind the frosted double doors.

Features:

  • Front mirrors and side mirrors
  • Ballet barres, wall-mounted

You MUST bring your student IDs with you to check in at the front desk!
