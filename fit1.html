Simmons FIT Studio 1

Holmes Sports Center

331 Brookline Ave. Boston, MA 02215


Getting there:

  • Located on the second floor of the Holmes Sports Center at the Simmons Residential campus.
  • Walk past the front desk and take a sharp left turn at the elevators.
  • Walk up one flight of stairs to the second floor, then take a left turn.
  • Walk through the set of double green doors.
  • FIT Studio 1 will be to your left.

Features:

  • Hardwood dance floor
  • Sound system with Bluetooth
  • Front mirrors
