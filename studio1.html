Simmons First Floor Dance Studio

Holmes Sports Center

331 Brookline Ave. Boston, MA 02215



Directions:

  • Located on the first floor of the Holmes Sports Center at the Simmons Residential campus.
  • Walk past the front desk and take a left turn.
  • Walk past the elevators and the dance studio will be inside the first set of double doors you enter.

Features:

  • Sprung hardwood dance floor with stage markers
  • Sound system with Bluetooth
  • Ballet barres, portable and wall-mounted
  • Front mirrors and side mirrors
